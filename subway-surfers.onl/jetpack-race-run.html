About Jetpack Race Run

Jetpack Race Run is a racing game as well as an arcade game with 3D stickman players and obstacle blocks. With a jetpack, you can fly to the last platform on a narrow path. If you keep dodging the impending barrier walls, you will be able to speed up and become the winner of each round. The game has no time or damage constraints.

This stickman is in deep space and he needs your help to get back home. Join him in his adventure and help him collect the stars across 6 different stages. Each stage has multiple pathways to reach the star, some easier than others. The paths change with every run, so you’ll have to be fast on your feet to reach all 6 stars before your time runs out! In this arcade game, you must avoid obstacles while running along a linear path (usually a track) towards the end point where a green means “Go” and red means “OUT”. A sticky trail will appear at random so you must be prepared for that or find another way to avoid it. 

Go as fast as you can to complete the course before the time runs out. Do you have what it takes? This is a game based on the famous rocket race video game,Stick man running 3D (SMB3D). Rocket Race Run is an endless runner game where you need to avoid obstacles and enemies while racing your rocket car through many different challenging levels. Avoid barrels, stones and other items that could make you crash. How long can you last? The faster you go,the harder it gets! Keep your eyes peeled for new obstacles and try to beat your own score! You can enjoy this game with a controller or a mouse so its perfect for everyone!

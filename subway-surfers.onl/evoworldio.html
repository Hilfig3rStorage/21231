About EvoWorld.io

Evoworldio is a new HTML5 multiplayer games where you have to keep the spawning animals from the plant from being eaten by each other. Avoid them if you can. You need to keep the animal population under control so that you don't run out of food. Evolution is the natural process by which species change over time. In this game, we have simulated this process and given it a name - Evolution! The plants and animals are in constant flux as they compete against one another for survival, reproduction and dominance. The more animals that you have on your side, the better your chances of survival!
